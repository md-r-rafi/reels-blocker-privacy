How Reels Blocker handles your data.

Effective: May 19, 2026 Last updated: May 19, 2026

At a glance

Reels Blocker collects no personal data. Everything runs entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any server we operate. Nothing is shared with third parties. There is no account, no analytics, and no advertising SDK.

1What this app does

Reels Blocker helps you avoid short-form video content (YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, TikTok) by detecting when such content appears on your screen and dismissing it. Detection happens locally on your device using Android's Accessibility Service.

2What we collect

Nothing. Reels Blocker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information whatsoever.

What stays on your device

The following data is created and stored only on your device, and never sent anywhere:

What we do NOT collect

We explicitly do not collect, log, or transmit:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any contact information
  • Your device ID, advertising ID, IMEI, or any unique identifier
  • Your IP address or location
  • The websites you visit or the apps you use
  • The content of any screen the app processes
  • Crash reports tied to any user identity
  • Analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking
  • Anything else

3Accessibility Service permission

Reels Blocker requires Android's Accessibility Service permission to detect short-form video content on your screen. This permission is powerful — it can read screen content. We use it for one purpose only: to detect Shorts/Reels containers and dismiss them.

Specifically, the Accessibility Service:

  • Reads the UI tree of the currently-foregrounded app to look for Shorts/Reels container view IDs (e.g. YouTube's reel_recycler resource)
  • Does NOT record what you watch, what you type, your passwords, or your messages
  • Does NOT take screenshots
  • Does NOT transmit anything off the device
  • Does NOT control your device beyond pressing the BACK button when short-form content is detected

The list of apps it monitors is hard-coded into the app itself (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and major Android browsers). It cannot be remotely configured because the app has no remote backend.

4Third-party services

Reels Blocker does not use any third-party SDK that collects user data. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any other analytics service
  • AdMob or any advertising network
  • Social-media SDKs (Facebook SDK, etc.)
  • Any user-identifying telemetry
  • Any remote crash-reporting service

5Children's privacy

Reels Blocker does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

6Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or export. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

7Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy in a meaningful way (for example, if we ever start collecting any data — which we do not currently plan to), we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify users via an in-app message before the change takes effect.
